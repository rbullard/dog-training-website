Frequently Asked Questions

What types of dogs do you train?

We work with dogs of all breeds, ages, and temperaments, including puppies, seniors, and dogs with behavioral challenges.

How long does training take?

The length of training depends on your dog’s needs and your goals. We will discuss a personalized plan during the initial consultation.

Do I need any special equipment?

We recommend a standard leash, a properly fitted collar, and high-value treats. We can guide you on equipment during the consultation.

Where do the training sessions take place?

Sessions are held in your home, local parks, or other agreed-upon locations to simulate real-world environments.

Can you help with aggressive dogs?

Yes, we have experience working with dogs that exhibit aggression. Safety is our top priority, and we will create a plan tailored to your dog’s needs.
